# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is committed to being a welcoming and respectful community for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or derogatory language.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Deliberate intimidation or unwelcoming conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private GitHub issue
or contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

## Enforcement

The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban participants
who violate this Code of Conduct.

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
